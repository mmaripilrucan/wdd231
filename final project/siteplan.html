Alquimia Financiera Consultores – Website Plan

Site Name

Alquimia Financiera Consultores

This name clearly identifies the professional service offered (financial consulting). It builds personal branding, trust, and local recognition—critical in the financial services industry where credibility is paramount.

Site Purpose

  • The site serves as a professional online presence for financial consulting practice.
  • It provides clear information about services (investment, tax, retirement, and estate planning).
  • It showcases client success stories, introduces the team, and offers a contact form for prospective clients to request a free consultation

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A person looking for advisory for retirement asks: What financial planning services do you offer for retirement?
  • Scenario 2: An executive from a company needs advisory and asks: Can I see real client testimonials before booking a consultation?

Color Schema

Deep Blue (#0056b3): Used for: Headings, navigation links (on hover/active), buttons, and key accents. Represents trust, stability, and professionalism.

Light Gray (#f8f9fa): Used for: Page background. Ensures readability and clean layout.

Typography

  • Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif – clean, modern, and elegant.
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – highly readable on all devices.
  • Code/Technical: Consolas, monospace – only for dev references.
